Data and settings

Your data never leaves this device unless you export it.
Backup matters. Bills are stored only in this browser. Clearing site data or switching device will lose them. Export a backup file regularly, and import it on another device to carry your data across.

Reconcile with bank statement

Confirm which bills actually went through.
Download a statement as CSV from your banking app, then load it here. The file is read in your browser and never uploaded anywhere. It is matched against your unpaid bills by amount so you can mark the ones that cleared.
